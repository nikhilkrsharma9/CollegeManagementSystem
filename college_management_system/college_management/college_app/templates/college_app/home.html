About Us

GGSESTC is a premier institution located in Bokaro, Jharkhand, offering quality education in engineering, management, and computer applications. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty, we aim to foster innovation, research, and holistic development among students.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical and professional education, fostering innovation, research, and entrepreneurship to create globally competent professionals who contribute to the advancement of society and industry.

Our Mission

To empower students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in a competitive global environment while nurturing ethical values, leadership qualities, and a spirit of innovation.

Infrastructure Details

Our campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a vast library, and high-speed internet connectivity. We also provide excellent residential facilities, sports amenities, and eco-friendly initiatives to ensure a holistic learning environment.
